ABOUT
ABOUT

DropChain is an end-to-end supply chain ecosystem addressing the need for transparency and traceability in the global food and beverage trade.

Food fraud is a $40 billion global problem, and consumers have demonstrated an increasing demand for safe and healthy foods. Trust has been lost over the years due to numerous food scandals around the world. That trust with consumers must be rebuilt through a more transparent and accountable food supply network.

DropChain leverages tokenized Blockchain technology to create an innovative gamified cryptoeconomic incentive program that rewards each physical distribution channel for their participation in the supply chain.

Driving full transparency in the food supply chain will empower brands with the laser-focused market data they've never had, in addition to addressing the food safety issues prevalent in developing countries today.

EXISTING BUSINESS
EXISTING BUSINESS

DropChain is addressing the need for transparency and traceability in the global food and beverage trade.


With an established market base in China and existing alcohol distribution platform, we’ve managed hundreds of unique brands and over 1000 SKU’s, selling to SME and corporate customers since 2015.

OUR SOLUTION
OUR SOLUTION

We are developing an end-to-end supply chain ecosystem targeting the $1.5T global food trade.


Powered by an industry-first gamified, tokenized incentive structure using blockchain technology, we reward physical distribution channels driving real participation and engagement in modern supply chains.

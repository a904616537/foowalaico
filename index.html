ABOUT
ABOUT

DropChain is the first alcohol-focused supply chain ecosystem addressing the need for transparency and traceability in the alcohol industry.

Current Blockchain solutions simply focus on distributing tokens to data centers but fail to realize humans are central to supply chains and individual incentives must be aligned along the way.

DropChain leverages tokenized Blockchain technology to create an innovative gamified cryptoeconomic incentive program that rewards each physical distribution channel for their participation in the supply chain.

This in turn will drive full transparency in the supply chain ecosystem, enabling big brands to acquire the laser-focused market data they need to make better business decisions. At the same time, it addresses the food safety issues prevalent in developing countries today, allowing consumers to trace a product from grape to glass.
